About IEEE MBITS

HISTORY OF IEEE MBITS

The IEEE Student Branch at Mar Baselios Institute of Technology and Science (MBITS) was established on 3rd July 2022 with an initial membership of 34 students. It was formed with the aim of promoting technical excellence, innovation, and professional development among students.

The branch was guided by Branch Counselor Prof. Robin George, Assistant Professor in the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE), and led by its first Student Chair, Dileep Kumar A. from the ECE Department. The official inauguration was conducted by Dr. Rajesh M. V., Chairman of the IEEE Kochi Subsection.

Since its inception, IEEE SB MBITS has launched three active Student Branch Chapters (SBCs): the IEEE Signal Processing Society (SPS), the IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) Affinity Group, and the IEEE Computer Society (CS). These chapters play a significant role in organizing workshops, technical talks, coding competitions, and hackathons.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is IEEE and why should I join? +

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for humanity. By joining, you gain access to cutting-edge research, networking opportunities, career resources, and technical publications that can significantly enhance your professional development.

How can I become a member of IEEE MBITS Student Branch? +

Students from MBITS can join by registering through our membership portal. You need to be enrolled at MBITS and have an interest in technology and engineering. Membership includes access to all our events, workshops, and resources.

What activities and events do you organize? +

We organize technical workshops, guest lectures from industry experts, hackathons, research projects, networking events, and innovation labs. We also participate in IEEE competitions and collaborate on entrepreneurship initiatives.

Are there any membership fees? +

IEEE Student membership has a nominal annual fee that provides access to IEEE resources including IEEE Xplore Digital Library, career guidance, and exclusive student benefits. The fee is significantly discounted for students.

Can I participate in research projects? +

Absolutely! We encourage student participation in research projects. We collaborate with faculty and industry partners on various research initiatives, and members can contribute to publications and present their work at conferences.
